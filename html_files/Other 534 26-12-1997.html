Spot the deliberate errors 

Author: DAVID WATTS

Date: 26-12-1997

 THE clever ones started writing even before the ink had dried on last Friday's paper. They had tried my holiday music quiz and confidently answered most questions.
But when they looked at the answers I supplied, oh, the horror. I said the 1910 Fruitgum Company sang Yummy, Yummy, Yummy (and Chewy, Chewy). They knew it was Ohio Express.
And you know what? They were right. They spotted my deliberate error, the product of a mind addled by actually dancing to those two songs while wearing a floral shirt and flares, and in the company of girls in hot pink and lime green, whose dresses flashed brightly under the pulsating strobe lights.
How could we forget that the 1910 Fruitgum Company was responsible for the priceless Indian Giver (Indian Givuh, Indian Givuh, You took your love away from me); or 1,2,3 Red Light?
