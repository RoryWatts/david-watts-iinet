Lost friends sought 

Author: DAVID WATTS

Date: 09-04-1996

 A YOUNG German backpacker has used the Internet to try to track down a Perth couple who befriended him and his partner in Yanchep last year.
Peter Reinhart, of Thalmassing, near Nuremberg, trekked around WA in a campervan in January 1995. He and partner Marion stopped at Yanchep lagoon and met a Perth couple who put them up for the night in their weekender.
The couple showed Mr Reinhart the delights of WA wildlife, including a mob of kangaroos at sunset.
The couples exchanged addresses but Mr Reinhart lost the Perth couple's details during wild weather in Germany late last year.
Determined to make contact with people who had gone out of their way to make their stay in WA pleasant, Mr Reinhart used Internet e-mail to find out who in WA could help him.
He tracked down my e-mail address and sent me a photograph of the Perth couple by more traditional means.
He would like the couple to contact him at Speihackerstrasse 6, 93107 Thalmassing, Germany.
