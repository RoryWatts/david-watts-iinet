More power to you 

Author: DAVID WATTS

Date: 12-08-1997

 The weather is crucial for everyone, not least Western Power, suppliers of the State's energy needs. Facts about Perth's climate average temperatures, rainfall, etc, are a new feature at Western Power's Web site. Consumers can also find copious information about power prices, products and services and more efficient energy use.
Two Perth sites are trying to provide comprehensive directories of WA businesses on the Web. The WA Internet Directory offers businesses who list with it a full Web page to display information, graphics and a link to the company's own site.
Businessweb offers a search engine to pick up WA companies and other sites who list with it.
A mouth-watering time awaits surfers who browse the Blue Holes site.It is about fish, especially the kind you catch and eat. Blue Holes promises to be a good resource for fishermen with links to weather all around the nation's coast, rigs, bait and, most importantly, the best fish to eat.
