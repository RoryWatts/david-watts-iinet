Geek be trendy, geek be chic 

Author: DAVID WATTS

Date: 11-06-1996

 CHIC rhymes with geek and there is now a World Wide Web move to find just how trendy Netizens have become.
The geekchic site in the US offers a 20-question quiz for surfers to find out whether they are drones, geeks or, the ultimate, geek chic.
Only those who can use Java with their browsers need bother ... this is how geeky you have to be just to get in the front door.
Mind you, the preponderance of new Net users with Windows95 and Netscape 2.0 or above actually means there could be more than a few ?newbies? stumbling on the geekchic quiz.
Surfers are asked to select one of three descriptions best describing their lifestyle and to click on that option.
The selection prompts a sign that flashes on the screen, saying either ?drone?, ?geek? or ?geek chic?.
At the bottom of the screen a line moves from ?drone? to ?geek chic?, depending on the answers.
For example the surfer is asked to nominate one of the following three as a turn-on:
?An IBM Thinkpad (response - geek).
? Sharon Stone or Mel Gibson (drone).
? An IBM Thinkpad on the laps of either Sharon Stone or Mel Gibson (geek chic).
It never gets more complicated than that.
