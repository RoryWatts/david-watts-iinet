Forget quiz nights, try this one 

Author: DAVID WATTS

Date: 19-12-1997

 THIS is the first of three quizzes to celebrate the holiday season. This week's is mainly about music because it is party time; next week will be TV because a lot of people will be slumped in front of it on Boxing Day, and the week after will be sport because I like sport ? so there.
Each quiz will start by asking you to supply the missing lyrics in a song. Here we go (please don't peek at the bottom because that is where the answers are:
1. You talk like -- -- and you dance like Zizi Jean-Maire.
Your clothes are all made by -- and there's -- and -- in your hair.
You live in a fancy apartment off the Boulevard -- --
Where you keep your -- -- records and a friend of Sasha --.
2. Who wrote and sang this celebration of late 60s nonsense?
3. What was his only other hit?
4. Who replaced Brian in the Rolling Stones after he drowned?
5. Name the members of The Monkees.
6. Which one of them starred in a late 50s children's TV series?
7. Name that show.
8. Fill in the missing words:
Yummy, --, -- I've got -- in my tummy.
9. Who was responsible for that work of high art?
10. What was their other hit about stomach functions?
11. Speaking of stomachs, which Spice Girl used to flash her's - and much more - in her previous career as a stripper and topless dancer?
12. Which one is known as Posh Spice?
13. Why?
14. Why does the group that sings the Barbie Song sound like the Chipmunks? (oh, you don't have to answer that, it was rhetorical).
15. Name the Chipmunks.
16. And their long-suffering manager?
17. What artist did the boys bop along to in the dole queue in the Full Monty?
18. True or false: Rolf Harris did a version of Stairway to Heaven?
19. Who did the original, which is one of the undisputed classics of rock music?
20. Name the singer.
21. Another undisputed rock classic is Born to Run. Who sang it?
22. What is his nickname?
23. Placido Domingo has just performed in Perth. Name the other two who with him constitute the world's greatest supergroup, the Three Tenors.
24. In which opera does a baritone bullfighter have a scene-stealing part?
25. Hum a few bars of his song (oh, you don't have to answer that either).
26. Fill in the missing words (yes again):
We've golden -- and wealth for toil
Our home is -- by sea.
27. Name that song.
28. Now stand up and sing it (done it again)
29. What song did champion boxer Lionel Rose have a hit with?
30. What about actor Richard Harris?
31. Did it make any sense at all? (you don't have to answer that either).
32. Fill in the missing words from this 60s song by The Kinks. If you get them you don't have to answer anything else. Your prize will be utter contentment that you are a genius. (Clue: this song was also a punk rock hit for either or both the Clash and the Cure. Not my genre):
I wish I could be like -- --.

Macarthur Park (Someone left a cake out in the rain etc) 31. Absolutely none 32. David Watts.
Merry Christmas.
